Introducing Take profile notes

I'm building Take profile notes , a chrome extension to help you effortlessly take notes and build a network databse of interesting people you find on LinkedIn.


The extension allows you do the following : Take notes on any Linkedin profile you visit. You can authorize the extension to create a google sheet, where the profile notes will be automatically synced You can update notes, and delete them from your google sheet. You can access the google sheetby clicking on View all notes on any Linkedin profile
