Privacy Policy for Take Profile Notes

Last updated: 2024-12-15

1. Introduction

Welcome to Take Profile Notes (the "Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension, which integrates with Google services to sync Take Profile notes to Google Sheets.

By using our Extension, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Google Account Information

When you connect your Google account via OAuth, we access the following information:

  • Google Sheets API access to create and update spreadsheets.
  • Drive API access to create new spreadsheets in your Google Drive.

We do not collect your email address or other personal account details.

2.2. LinkedIn URL and Notes

You provide LinkedIn URLs and associated notes that are stored locally in your browser's storage and synced to a Google Spreadsheet in your account.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Create new spreadsheets in your Google Drive if no spreadsheet exists.
  • Sync LinkedIn URLs and notes to your designated Google Spreadsheet.
  • Update existing rows in the spreadsheet when notes are modified.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: LinkedIn URLs and notes are stored in your browser's local storage.
  • Google Drive: Data is stored in your Google Spreadsheet within your Google Drive.

We do not store your data on any external servers.

We use OAuth 2.0 for secure authorization and access to your Google account. Your OAuth token is securely managed by Chrome's Identity API.

5. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not share or sell your data with any third parties.

We only use Google's APIs (Sheets) to enable core functionalities of the Extension

6. Your Control Over Data

You can:

  • Revoke access to your Google account at any time through your Google Account settings: Google Permissions Page.
  • Delete local data by clearing your Chrome extension's local storage.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We store your personal information for a period of time that is consistent with our business purposes. When the data retention period expires for a given type of data, we will delete or destroy it. You can delete your data by uninstalling the chrome extension, this deletes and destroys all the data you have added on the application, no copy of this is retained by the application

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Last updated" date. Please review this policy occasionally for updates.
