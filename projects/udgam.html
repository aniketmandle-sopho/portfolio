@ Udgam, the Annual Entrepreneurship Summit

Udgam 2019

UDGAM was conceptualized to develop and spread the spirit of entrepreneurship amongst the youth of India, especially in the North-East. Our mission is aptly illustrated through the tagline, “Conceive, Believe, Achieve.” Despite its humble beginnings, UDGAM is the biggest Entrepreneurship-Summit of North-East India and easily one of the pinnacle E-summits in India.
UDGAM is working to revolutionise the entrepreneurship culture in North East India which is young but growing at a rapid pace by creating a wave through the networking opportunities, Disrupt, Strategy Storm, Lecture Series, Intern Fair, Product Hackathon, Workshops, Panel Discussion, Networking Lunch and various other crafted events offered as a part of UDGAM. With a whole range of events lined up to foster innovation and a questioning mindset, along with a mammoth reach of hundreds of colleges and schools from all over the Nation, it is a summit which anyone with the slightest inclination towards entrepreneurship would not want to miss.
